About Us

HEXAIVITA '25

A National-level symposium

The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Sethu Institute of Technology is thrilled to host the National symposium, "Explainable AI in Healthcare", proudly sponsored by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB).

This event serves as a dynamic platform uniting researchers, academicians, healthcare professionals, and AI enthusiasts to delve into the transformative role of Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) in healthcare. As AI continues to revolutionize medical diagnostics, treatment planning, and patient care, this symposium emphasizes the critical need for transparency, trust, and accountability in AI-driven healthcare solutions.

Date : 6 & 7 February 2025

SERB

Science and Engineering Research Board

The Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB) is a statutory body established under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. Committed to advancing research in science, engineering, and allied disciplines, SERB serves as a premier funding agency that supports cutting-edge projects and innovations.

With a vision to foster excellence and promote India's global competitiveness in research and development, SERB provides grants, fellowships, and initiatives that empower researchers, academicians, and institutions across the country. The board is known for facilitating impactful collaborations, nurturing talent, and addressing critical challenges through robust and transparent support mechanisms.

SERB's sponsorship of our symposium highlights their dedication to empowering young minds and promoting innovation, making a significant contribution to the development of future scientists and engineers.
